Hi my name is Sharm and I have some advice for you!

Programming is magical.

                

Programming the crossroads of creativity and innovation. Beginning as a conceptual idea, programming allows you to translate ideas from the mind into concrete, physical results.

                  

Therefore, whatever you dream of, you can create! You are the ultimate end result of your creation.

               

Programming is a metaphor for life.

                

Life has all sorts of obstacles and challenges. With programming, you can test many different options before coming to a conclusion to what works best. The same thing goes for life. There are my paths to choose and a lot of different directions to take. With programming you’re forced to make choice and in doing so, you can conquer decision paralysis.

                  

Programming is synonymous with life because with every decision you make, good or bad, you get to learn from your choices and build your experiences.

                  

In the end, it doesn’t matter if the choices were “right” or “wrong” but how far you have come from the beginning of your journey.
